TRANSFER

TRANSFRT take a series of random integer number. Those number should be rearrange their position according to the string that following. c or C represent column and number should be arrange line by line, which is the column style. r or R represent row and number should be arrange in a row style.


Input: TRANSFER 1 4 3 5  c
Output: 
1
4
3
5

Input: TRANSFER 4 6 8 3 5 R
Output: 4 6 8 3 5
